How to Perform
1. Tilt phone to pitch angle for the suit
2. Tap the value hotspot (A–K) — flashes suit + vibrates to confirm
3. Tap Enter hotspot → WhatCard opens
4. Hand phone to spectator
5. Take phone back, shake to reveal
6. Triple back-tap to reset & return home
Suit pitch: 0–22°=♠ · 23–44°=♥ · 45–67°=♣ · 68–90°=♦
